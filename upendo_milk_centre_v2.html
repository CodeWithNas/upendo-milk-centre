Our Story

About Upendo Milk Centre

"Upendo" means love

Upendo Milk Centre is a family-run dairy business founded in 2006, formerly known as Amani Milk Centre. The business continues the dairy knowledge and leadership legacy of its founder, Rev. Kennedy Stephen Kisanga — and places love at the centre of everything it makes: its products, its customer relationships, its community service, and its purpose.

  • ✓Affordable and nutritious dairy products
  • ✓High standards of cleanliness and food safety
  • ✓Support for local families, businesses, and institutions
  • ✓Community empowerment and job creation
  • ✓A commitment to reliable service and consistent product quality
